# Department Representatives

Each department in the Graduate School may nominate one GSC representative with voting privileges. Representatives are chosen by each department — usually through a departmental election or appointment by the Director of Graduate Studies (DGS).

## What do department reps do?

- Attend monthly General Body Meetings (first Tuesday of each month, 6–7pm, Alumni Hall 201)
- Vote on GSC resolutions and business
- Relay graduate student concerns from their department to the GSC
- Pass information about GSC events and resources back to their department

## Is your department represented?

The current department rep list is maintained in the [GSC Box folder](https://vanderbilt.box.com/s/crffhvfojzjvgwv7jia5n0gwzyzyjd97). Contact [gsc@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gsc@vanderbilt.edu) if:

- Your department is not listed
- Your department's rep listing is out of date
- You'd like to become your department's rep

## At-large voting members

Additional voting members may be appointed by a vote of the general body. These at-large members may represent departments in addition to the department representative, or may represent a graduate student constituency not associated with a specific department (e.g., a graduate student organization). Nominations can come from self-nomination or from other members.

## How to become a rep

Contact your department's Director of Graduate Studies, or reach out to the GSC at [gsc@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gsc@vanderbilt.edu). There's no application — departments decide their own selection process.
