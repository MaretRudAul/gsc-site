# Campus Resources for Graduate Students

The following offices serve graduate students directly. The GSC works with many of them through the **Graduate Development Network (GDN)**, a monthly coordinating group administered by the Graduate School.

---

## Academic & Career Support

**Graduate and Postdoc Academic Success (GPAS)**
Workshops, programming, and individual success planning sessions covering progress, productivity, and navigating academic relationships.
- Director: Stacey Satchell · [gpas@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gpas@vanderbilt.edu)
- Program Manager: Nick Hyer
- Location: Alumni Hall, 3rd floor

**Career Center — Graduate & Postdoctoral Success Team**
Career coaching, employer connections, and resources for graduate students and postdocs (up to 2 years post-graduation).
- [Career Center website](https://career.vanderbilt.edu)
- Location: Student Life Center, 2nd Floor

**Writing Studio**
Feedback and coaching on writing at any stage of the process. Open to all Vanderbilt community members.
- Director: John Bradley
- Location: Curb Center Building, 1801 Edgehill Ave, Suite 112

**Heard Libraries**
Research support, library access, and student engagement programming.
- Student Engagement Librarian: Leslie Foutch
- Location: Central Library, 419 21st Ave S

**English Language Center**
Academic English and cultural support for students using English as an additional language.
- Associate Director: Jim Cracraft
- Location: Zeppos College, 1100 Suite

## Health & Wellbeing

**Student Care Network**
Umbrella for all health and wellness services. Includes:
- **Student Care Coordination (SCC)** — connects students to the right resources
- **University Counseling Center (UCC)** — mental health services *(note: wait times can be long; plan ahead)*
- **Student Health Center (SHC)** — primary care
- **Center for Student Wellbeing (CSW)** — wellness programming (underutilized but highly available)

**Project Safe**
Information, support, and education around sexual and intimate partner violence.
- Location: 2304H Vanderbilt Place

## Identity & Community Centers

**Bishop Joseph Johnson Black Cultural Center (BCC)**
Programming and resources for Black students, faculty, and staff.
- Director: ReChard Peel
- Location: 388 24th Ave S *(coordinates: 36.14573°N, 86.80305°W — GPS results vary)*

**Margaret Cuninggim Women's Center**
Affirming space and programming addressing sexism, racism, homophobia, and other forms of oppression.
- Director: Rory Dicker
- Location: 2304D Vanderbilt Place, Franklin House

**KC Potter Center (LGBTQI Life)**
Cultural center and resource for information and support around gender and sexuality.
- Location: 2304E Vanderbilt Place

**International Student & Scholar Services (ISSS)**
Immigration advising, counseling, and cross-cultural programming for international students.
- Director: Julie Wilbers
- Location: 1107 18th Ave S

**Center for Spiritual and Religious Life (CSRL)**
Interfaith programming, spiritual support, and community for students navigating faith and identity.
- Location: 2304G Vanderbilt Pl *(coordinates: 36.144390°N, 86.804169°W — GPS results vary)*

## Housing & Transportation

**Housing and Relocation Coordinator**
Helps faculty, staff, postdocs, and graduate students find housing.
- Kayla Austin · [kayla.m.austin@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:kayla.m.austin@vanderbilt.edu)

**MoveVU**
