# Graduate Student Organizations

The GSC works with graduate student organizations across Vanderbilt and maintains connections with organization leaders through the Vice President of Internal Affairs. The transition document notes that the VP of Internal Affairs should connect with leaders of graduate student organizations and maintain the relevant spreadsheet in the GSC Box folder.

## Looking for the current directory?
The current graduate student organization list is maintained internally by the GSC. To ask about the current directory, connect your organization with the GSC, or update organization information, email gsc@vanderbilt.edu .

## Graduate student organizations and GSC representation
In addition to department representatives, the GSC may appoint additional voting members. These at-large voting members may represent a graduate student constituency not represented by a department, including a graduate student organization.

## Funding and co-sponsorships
Graduate student organizations may also be eligible for GSC co-sponsorships and for graduate and professional student organization funding processes administered in coordination with the Dean of Students Office. See the Funding pages for details.
