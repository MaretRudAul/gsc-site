# GSC Meetings

## General Body Meetings (GBMs)

**When:** First Tuesday of each month, 6–7pm  
**Where:** Alumni Hall 201 (also available on Zoom via OWL)  
**Who:** Open to all graduate students — no registration required

GBMs are where the full council meets: executive board, department representatives, at-large members, and any interested graduate students. The agenda typically includes:

- Updates from the executive board and committees
- Presentations from guest speakers (Graduate School, GPAS, other offices)
- Votes on resolutions and business items
- Q&A and open discussion

**Minutes and slides** from past GBMs are public and available in the [GSC Box folder](https://vanderbilt.box.com/s/crffhvfojzjvgwv7jia5n0gwzyzyjd97) and linked from [Governance Documents](/docs/policies/governance-documents).

## Executive Board Meetings

**When:** Monthly (typically last Tuesday of the month), 6–7:30pm  
**Who:** Executive Board members only (unless guests are invited)

Executive board meetings are for internal coordination and planning. The President and VPs set the agenda and distribute it to the board and faculty advisor in advance.

---

## Joining on Zoom

GBMs are hybrid. The OWL camera/speaker system is used to include remote participants. Zoom link is distributed via AnchorLink email before each meeting.
