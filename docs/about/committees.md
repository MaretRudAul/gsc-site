# Programs & Committees

## 3 Minute Thesis (3MT) {#3-minute-thesis-3mt}

The 3 Minute Thesis competition challenges graduate students to present their research in plain language — to a general audience — in just three minutes, with a single static slide.

**Open to:** All PhD and research master's students in the Graduate School

**Frequency:** Once per academic year (spring semester)

**Led by:** Academic Chairs

:::info Start early
Past academic chairs note that 3MT requires roughly 5× more preparation than expected and takes 10× more time than anticipated. The earlier you contact the academic chairs about participating, the better.
:::

**Key contact:** [gsc_academic@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gsc_academic@vanderbilt.edu)  
**Senior Admin contact:** [kaitlyn.o.garrett@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:kaitlyn.o.garrett@vanderbilt.edu)

## Travel Grants {#travel-grants}

Monthly lottery-based funding for graduate students attending conferences, workshops, or professional development events. Awarded August–May (10 grants per year).

See the full details on the [Travel Grant page](/docs/resources/travel-support).

## Dinner with Dores {#dinner-with-dores}

A community dining program connecting graduate students with each other and the broader Vanderbilt community. Typically held 2–3 times per academic year.

**Led by:** Community Chairs  
**Contact:** [gsc_community@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gsc_community@vanderbilt.edu)

## De-Stress & Decompress {#de-stress-decompress}

A wellness event held each fall (typically early November) offering graduate students a chance to relax and decompress during the semester.

**Led by:** Community Chairs

## Social Events {#social-events}

The Social Chairs plan 1–2 major social events per semester (typically 100+ attendees) plus smaller auxiliary events throughout the year. These are open to all graduate students and are a great way to meet people outside your department.

Watch for announcements on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/vu_graduate_student_council/) and via AnchorLink email.

**Contact:** [gsc_social@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gsc_social@vanderbilt.edu)

## Standing Committees

The VP of Internal Affairs oversees several standing committees. Current active committees include:

- **Health Insurance Committee** — reviews and provides graduate student input on the Student Health Insurance Plan (SHIP). The GSC President recommends 1–2 graduate students to serve on the university SHIP committee in years when the insurance contract is up for renewal.
- **Housing Committee**
- **Data Committee**
- **International Student Committee**
- **Ad Hoc Committees** — formed as needed for specific issues

Graduate students interested in serving on a committee should contact [gsc@vanderbilt.edu](mailto:gsc@vanderbilt.edu) or the VP of Internal Affairs.

---

## Graduate Student Survey

The GSC administers an independent graduate student survey (separate from any university surveys). It was first administered in 2022–23 and again in spring 2025. Survey data is used to inform advocacy efforts and presentations to administrators including the Provost and College Deans.

Survey data is kept confidential — only GSC survey committee members and executive board have access to raw data. Aggregate results are shared publicly at General Body Meetings.
