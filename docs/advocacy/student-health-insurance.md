# Student Health Insurance (SHIP)

Graduate student health care is administered under the Student Health Insurance Plan (SHIP). SHIP covers eligible undergraduate, professional, and graduate students through a single university contract with a health insurance company. Changes to SHIP are negotiated through the SHIP committee during the year before a contract takes effect.

## The GSC’s role
Graduate students are usually allotted one or two positions on the SHIP committee, and those students are recommended by the GSC President. In years when the committee is meeting, the Dean of Students Office typically contacts the GSC President in late fall or early spring about filling those graduate student positions. In other years, the current contract remains in effect and no action is needed.

## Committee confidentiality
Students selected for the SHIP committee may be asked by the Dean of Students Office to keep committee deliberations confidential until a contract is signed. Because health insurance can be a sensitive and important issue for graduate students, this can place student representatives in a difficult position. In some circumstances, the President may choose to keep the appointed graduate student representative or representatives anonymous.

## Questions
For questions about the GSC’s role in student health insurance, contact gsc@vanderbilt.edu .
