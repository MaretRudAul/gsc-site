# Graduate Student Survey

The Graduate Student Council administers an independent Graduate Student Survey separate from university surveys. According to the transition document, the survey was first administered in the 2022–2023 academic year and was administered again in Spring 2025. Survey results help inform GSC advocacy and have been presented at General Body Meetings and in meetings with administrators, including the Provost and College Deans.

## Data privacy
Only graduate students on the GSC survey committee and executive board should have access to the raw survey data. Raw data should not be shared with other graduate students or administrators. When data is presented publicly, including at GBMs, it must be sufficiently anonymized.

## Survey platform
The GSC survey committee elected to use REDCap to administer the survey. The transition document notes that REDCap is secure and robust, but less user-friendly than simpler survey tools.

## Why this matters
The transition document emphasizes that graduate students place a very high level of trust in the GSC when participating in the survey, and that survey governance should reflect that trust.
