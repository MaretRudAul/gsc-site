## About the GSC

### Mission
The Graduate Student Council (GSC) is the representative body that serves students of the Graduate School. The Graduate School, and thus the GSC, serves some research-focused master’s programs and all Ph.D. programs at Vanderbilt University.

**Primary Goals**
- Coordinate academic, social, and community activities.
- Represent graduate student opinions and facilitate communication with administration and faculty.
